Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

  • Without an account: Nothing. Preferences and history stay in your browser's localStorage.
  • With an account: Your username, watch history, and saved titles stored securely via our auth service.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not use cookies beyond authentication

Third-Party Services

  • TMDB — metadata API
  • Google Fonts — font delivery
  • Cloudflare Workers — auth service
  • Video embed providers — AnimeNexus has no control over their data practices

Data Retention

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings. localStorage can be cleared from your browser at any time.
